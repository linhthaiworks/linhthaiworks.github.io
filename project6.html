Lost in the pixels

Poster Collection

This poster project explores the sense of confusion that exists in the virtual world, where we consume endless content and overload our brains with information. It reflects the impact of information overload on personal identity and mental clarity. Each poster contains pixelated patterns and information that represent this feeling, which increases as you go along.
