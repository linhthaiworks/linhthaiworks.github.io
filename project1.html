Ai Wei Wei

Artist's Box

Artist Ai Weiwei's boxed set includes a poster, a book of his works, a secret file about his time in prison and a deck of cards and a pack of stickers. This box set is a dive into his powerful art and activism, bringing to light themes of freedom, human rights and resistance. It's a deeper look at how his personal experiences have shaped his global impact.
